Game Instructions

Each player will take turn to roll and they have the option to pass. To win the game, the player must get total points of 100 or all of the dices have the same number. If one of the dice is 1, it will pass to another player automatically. If the sum of three dices are less than or equal to 6, the score the player earned for that turn before passing will be deducted.
